# Grants and Funding

> [!NOTE]
> **Future Vision & Roadmap**: Grants, treasury matching, and platform-wide funding features are planned for future phases. The platform's base payment systems have been set up to support these features as the community grows.

Mosaic aims to provide sustainable ways to fund creative work. We are building tools for Villages to coordinate sponsorships, manage shared funds, and allocate grants to active projects.

## How Funding Will Work

### 1. Village Treasuries
Every Village can set up a transparent treasury address. Members can support the treasury through:
- **Subscriptions**: Regular contributions (in ADA) that unlock premium features or community-defined rights.
- **Direct Support**: One-time contributions to the general fund.

### 2. Micro-Grants
Village administrators or community voters can dedicate a portion of their treasury to micro-grants.
- **Proposals**: Creators submit a project proposal within the Village.
- **Review**: Members discuss the impact, scope, and requested budget.
- **Disbursement**: Approved grants are sent directly to the creator's registered Cardano wallet.

## Platform Grants Program
Mosaic plans to host platform-wide rounds to match contributions to emerging Villages.
- **Matching Pools**: We plan to use matching pools where the number of unique contributors matters more than the raw amount contributed, ensuring broad support for popular ideas.
- **Activity Verification**: Only users with verified contribution history can participate in matching rounds, preventing bots from gaming the system.
