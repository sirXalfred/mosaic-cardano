# Community Guidelines

Mosaic is a network of collaborative spaces designed for creators, writers, developers, and thinkers. To maintain a productive and respectful environment, all users must follow these guidelines.

## Core Principles

### 1. Respect and Inclusivity
We support diverse groups from all over the world. Treat other members with dignity. Harassment, hate speech, and discrimination will not be tolerated.

### 2. Intellectual Property and Attribution
Mosaic is designed to track how work is built.
- Always attribute contributors accurately.
- Do not copy or publish work that you do not have the rights to use.
- Honor collaborative connections—if you are building on someone else's work, link back to their original pieces when possible.

### 3. Safety and Security
- Do not share sensitive personal information (doxxing).
- Do not deploy malicious code, spam, or phishing attempts.
- Maintain wallet security—never share your private keys or seed phrases with anyone. Mosaic will never ask for them.

## Enforcement
Individual Villages set their own local moderation policies, managed by Village admins. However, system-wide terms are enforced by platform admins:
- **First violation**: Warning and temporary suspension of posting privileges.
- **Repeat violations**: Permanent ban from the platform and removal of user profile.

Each Village is empowered to manage its own space. Use progressive settings to collectively decide on guidelines and moderation limits.
