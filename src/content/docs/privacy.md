# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

At Mosaic, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Public Wallet Addresses**: When you link a Cardano wallet, we store your public address to associate with your profile, contributions, and subscriptions.
- **Profile Data**: Usernames, bios, profile images, and email addresses (if provided).
- **Activity logs**: Subscriptions, posts, contributions, and community memberships stored in our Neo4j database to power your Explore Feed and notifications.

## 2. How We Use Information
- To authenticate you via cryptographic signatures.
- To maintain your public Contribution Ledger and Reputation Passport.
- To deliver real-time notifications (via Web Push) and handle treasury routing.
- To improve platform usability and personalize content discovery.

## 3. Data Storage & Blockchain Immutability
- **Off-chain Database**: Profile data, posts, and notifications are stored in our secure database. You can request deletion of this personal database data.
- **On-chain Records**: Any data committed to the Cardano blockchain (such as payment records, minting transactions, or on-chain badges) is immutable and cannot be deleted or modified. By using the platform, you acknowledge this aspect of blockchain ledger technology.

## 4. Third-Party Integrations
We use secure image hosting (Cloudinary) for user uploads and the Cardano network to verify wallet transactions. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 5. Security
We implement industry-standard measures to safeguard your session data, including secure nonces, HTTP-only cookies, and encrypted backend communications.

For questions, feel free to submit feedback via the Support & Feedback dialog in the footer.
