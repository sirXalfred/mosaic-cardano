# Hackathon Playbook: Campus Innovators

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to the official Campus Innovators Hackathon Playbook. This guide is a collaboratively maintained handbook covering everything from early-stage planning to post-event wrap-ups. Whether you are a first-time organizer or a seasoned veteran, this playbook provides the standardized templates and checklists you need.

## 2. Pre-Event Planning (Months 1-3)
- **Define the Theme:** Choose a compelling and relevant theme (e.g., Sustainable Tech, AI for Good).
- **Venue Procurement:** Secure a location that can support 24/7 access, reliable Wi-Fi, and adequate power delivery.
- **Budgeting & Sponsorship:** Use the attached budget template. Target local businesses and tech companies for sponsorships. Offer tiered sponsorship packages (Bronze, Silver, Gold).
- **Date Selection:** Ensure the dates do not clash with major exams or other significant campus events.

## 3. Marketing & Outreach (Months 4-5)
- **Website Launch:** Deploy the registration portal at least 6 weeks before the event.
- **Social Media Campaign:** Utilize our standard graphic templates for Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
- **Campus Ambassadors:** Recruit representatives from various departments to spread the word offline.

## 4. Day-of Logistics
- **Registration & Swag:** Set up multiple check-in lines categorized by last name. Distribute name badges and sponsor swag bags upon entry.
- **Opening Ceremony:** Keep it under 45 minutes. Cover rules, judging criteria, and API presentations from sponsors.
- **Mentorship:** Designate a specific "Mentor Hub" where participants can go for technical help. Use a ticketing system for mentor requests.
- **Food & Beverage:** Plan for dietary restrictions (vegan, gluten-free, halal). Ensure coffee and energy drinks are stocked 24/7.

## 5. Judging & Prizes
- **Judging Criteria:** Projects are evaluated on Technical Complexity, Innovation, UI/UX Design, and Applicability to the Theme.
- **Demo Format:** Implement a science-fair style exposition rather than on-stage presentations to save time and reduce pressure.
- **Prizes:** Ensure prize distribution is transparent. Hardware prizes must be ordered 3 weeks in advance.

## 6. Post-Event Wrap-up
- **Feedback Survey:** Send out a participant survey within 24 hours of the event closing.
- **Sponsor Thank You:** Send personalized thank you notes and a post-event analytics report to all sponsors.
- **Retrospective:** Hold a core team meeting to discuss what went well and what needs improvement for next year.
